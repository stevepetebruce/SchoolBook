Just as the year was dipping back down into winter, the nights drawing longer and longer shadows, the residents of the secret house had patiently resigned themselves to the idea that would be spending another winter hidden away, keeping quiet, not knowing when it would be safe for Jewish people to walk around freely again.

Anne was quietly packing up a game she was playing, putting the cards back into...

CRRAAAUUUSSSHHHZZZTTT!!!!

There was a loud crash and the jagged sound of breaking wood. Men shouting, heavy boots rushing up stairs and a gang of uniformed men rushed out of the secret passage shouting and waving guns at all the people in the room.

Hitler's men had found them.
