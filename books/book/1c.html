They were an ancient, proud people who worked hard and helped out their friends and neighbours.

Before Anne was old enough to understand the things on the news, Otto was worried when he found out that a gang of angry men had come to power in their home town. Called the Nazis, they were led by a twisted evil man, Hitler, who hated Jewish people like Otto and his family. At first, after this gang came to power, nothing much changed. Then week by week, things got worse and worse for the Frank family and other Jews.

There were attacks on Otto's friends and families' houses and shops. Their windows were smashed, the children were picked on and the adults were beaten up. After a few weeks of these attacks, the time had come where it was no longer safe for Otto, Anne and the rest of the family to live in their own home town. Otto and his wife decided to move their daughters out of town, somewhere safe.
