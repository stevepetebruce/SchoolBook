Unknown to Anne and Margot, Hitler's enemies, "The Alliance", had been busy planning to destroy him and his men. Whilst Anne and her family spent years hiding in the secret house, The Alliance were building up their armies with men and weapons to fight Hitler's armies. While the Frank family were being moved to Camp A, The Alliance started their attack on Hitler's soldiers. Gigantic battles on the land, in the air and at sea were being fought and Hitler was losing.

More and more of the countries he had invaded were being won back by The Alliance who were making the people free again. The Alliance were driving further and further into the continent, destroying more and more of Hitler's soldiers and were soon close to Camp B where they could free all the people who had been imprisoned there.
