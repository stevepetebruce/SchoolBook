Unknown to Anne and Margot, Hitler's enemies, The Alliance, had been busy planning to destroy him and his men. Whilst Anne and her family spent years hiding in the secret house, The Alliance were building up their armies with men and weapons to fight Hitler's armies. While the Frank family were being moved to Camp A, The Alliance started their attack on Hitler's soldiers. Gigantic battles on the land, at sea and in the air were being fought and Hitler was losing.

More and more of the countries he had invaded were being won back by The Alliance who were making the people free again. The Alliance drove further and further into the continent, destroying more and more of Hitler's soldiers and were soon close to Camp B where they could free all the people who had been imprisoned there.

The Alliance soldiers finally beat Hitler's soldiers who were guarding Camp B and they could not believe what they saw: thousands of dead people lying in giant graves, with thousands more ill and starving, who were close to death themselves.
