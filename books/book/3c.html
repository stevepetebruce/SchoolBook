That night, they all walked the few hundred metres from their home to their new hiding place being careful not to be seen. They quietly entered the building, went to the top of the business offices, and pulled the bookcase away from the wall. One by one they squeezed up the narrow secret passage into their new hiding place and finally Otto pulled the bookcase back against the wall.

In the first floor room they took off their heavy jackets and dumped their belongings on the floor and took a moment to take in their new, secret home.

Days went by - very, very quiet days in their secret home. Only six people in the downstairs business offices knew that there was a whole family hiding upstairs. These six people would be the family's helpers, bringing them food and newspapers and books and games for the children. During the day, when the other workers were downstairs, they had to walk round on tip toes so not to make a noise
