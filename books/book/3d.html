After a week the Franks were joined by their friends, the van Pels family: Mr and Mrs van Pels and their son Peter. And then, months later they were joined by another friend, Fritz. In the end there was: Otto, Edith, Anne, Margot, Mr and Mrs van Pels, Peter AND Fritz - EIGHT people living in each other's pockets, day and night, every day of the week, in the secret house.
