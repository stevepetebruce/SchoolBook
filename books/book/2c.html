They flew slowly over to the battle and the people could hear the rumble of their engines as they approached. They had never seen anything like this before and they could not of known what damage the giant bombs in these planes could do. The planes drew closer and closer to their target city, which was miles from where the Frank family lived, but other families scared by these new weapons ran inside to hide from their attack. The planes were now over their target of the city. The pilots opened the bay doors and released the bombs.

Thousands of bombs were dropped and destroyed the heart of the city. The bombs and the fires they caused destroyed tens of thousands of peoples homes, making them homeless. This was too much. The people surrounded to Hitler's army and Otto, Edith, Margot and Anne, like everyone else, worried what now happen to them now Hitler was in power of their home.

At first, after the fighting stopped, nothing much changed. It was just quiet.
